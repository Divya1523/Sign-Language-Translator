About the Project

This project is built to recognize sign language gestures in real-time using a webcam. It translates the gestures into corresponding letters or words, forms meaningful sentences, and even speaks them out loud. Our goal is to bridge the communication gap for the hearing and speech impaired by using AI-powered gesture recognition.
